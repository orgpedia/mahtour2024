Preamble: - Hindavi Swarajya, founded by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, symbolizes the highest values of MN, self-respect, patriotism and sacrifice. Numerous Mavalis have sacrificed their lives for the creation and protection of this Swarajya.
During the historic meeting between Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and Afzal Khan at the base of Fort Pratapgad, Veer Jivaji Mahale saved the life of the Maharajah by killing the Sayyid rebellion against Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj at the right time. The history of Hindavi Swaraj was preserved by this unparalleled noise and timeliness of Bir Jivaji Mahale. The subsequent statement "Shiva was saved as a living being," which is immortalized in history, testifies to his immortal valour and continues to inspire every Marathi mind. Therefore, every year on the 10th of November, the Shiv Pratap Diwas ceremony is celebrated with great enthusiasm at Fort Pratapgad. Lakhs of domestic and foreign tourists, history researchers, students and Shiv lovers visit Pratapgad Fort every year for this ceremony. Therefore, the historical, cultural and tourist significance of Fort Pratapgad is increasing day by day.
To perpetuate the unparalleled valour of Bir Jivaji Mahale, various social and historical research institutes, Shiv Premiere Associations, as well as the Mahasabha, have set up a grand memorial to him at the base of Fort Pratapgad. There were constant demands from the people's representatives. In accordance with this demand, to underline the legacy of valour of Veer Jivaji Mahale, Ma.
