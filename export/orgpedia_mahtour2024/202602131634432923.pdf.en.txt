The Proposal: -
Advocates are required to be appointed as professional advisors to effectively safeguard the government's interest in various legal matters of the department as well as to obtain competent expert legal advice in court cases. The appointment is expected to be made in a transparent, fair and quality-based manner. Accordingly, the government was considering setting up a competent committee to scrutinise the application, verify eligibility, evaluate experience and make final recommendations for selecting and empanelling the candidates.
The Government's Decisions:
Department of Tourism and Cultural Affairs. The public notice dated 06.092025 and the following. The application of the legal practitioner has been received in accordance with the provisions of the advertisement given on 08.10.2025. Out of this application, a panelist of 2 buckets is to be selected. A committee of field officers under the Cultural Sub-Division is being constituted as follows to select a suitable professional from the application received as per the direction given by the Secretary (Cultural Affairs).
